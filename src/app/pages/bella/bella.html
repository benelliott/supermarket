Perspective 1: Bella

07:46

Bella, a busy financial services executive, pours a small glass of orange juice to drink with her breakfast - organic oatmeal with agave and chia seeds. She shakes the carton and finds it now to be empty.

“Jenny, add orange juice to the shopping list”.

The touchscreen on Bella’s smart fridge illuminates and on it appears Jenny, the cartoonish avatar of JenSys’ popular home assistant. Next to Jenny is a digital shopping list, whose graphics have a handwritten affectation. “That’s done. Your updated shopping list is on-screen now. Please make sure you’ve added everything before we send it to your Shopping Buddy at 7pm”.

“That’s everything.”

“Got it! Have a great day, Bella. Don’t forget the Q4 presentation is taking place in the Brooker Room at 11.”

Bella removes the reusable sleeve from the carton, placing it aside for when the new batch comes later. SweetSqueeze, her favourite brand, has chosen to adorn the sleeve with an enticing depiction of a pyramid of juicy oranges next to a tall glass of freshly poured juice.

Unsleeved, the biodegradable carton is just the usual indecipherable mishmash of barcodes and symbols. Bella drops it into the composting inlet under the sink and grabs her coat.

20:04

Bella sits in the lounge, reviewing fiscal reports on her tablet while the LiveWall plays the news in the background. Both devices softly chime and the news pauses itself. Jenny appears in a small bubble.

“Bella, your Shopping Buddy has arrived at the front door. Please quote the passcode ‘Enjoyment’ when accepting the delivery.”

Bella ambles to the porch. She opens the front door to be greeted by the sweaty face of a man twice her age, mere inches from her own. He’s panting, standing so close that she averts her eyes out of awkwardness. “Enjoyment,” she mutters. The man blinks, briefly uncomprehending, before nodding once.

The JennyBot awakens from its charging station under the stairs and silently wheels over to the teeming food crate the man has just dropped at the doorstep. Magnets connect the two and the Bot wheels the food into the kitchen, returning briskly with the empty crate from last week.

“Thank you. Have a lovely evening,” the breathless man manages, last week’s crate under one arm, already half-way back to the e-bike he left on the pavement.

Bella shuts the door and slipper-shuffles back to her spot on the sofa. Jenny pops up one last time.

“Bella, we hope you had a great shopping experience this evening. Check your tablet now to review tipping options for your Shopping Buddy. Please be assured that the four-minute delay has been acknowledged and a coupon has been credited to your account. We apologise for the delay and are taking steps to ensure it won’t happen again”.

Bella resumes the news broadcast and turns the volume up to drown out the sound of the Bot working in the kitchen - carefully unpacking each item in the crate, placing it in its reusable branded sleeve and organising the kitchen storage optimally to suit Bella’s busy lifestyle.

Bella dismisses the tipping options on her tablet and gets back to work.
